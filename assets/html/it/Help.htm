PAD UPDATE GUIDE

PAD Description：

   PAD based on the Android platform

PAD Update：

   Two update modes are supported at present:

   1) Local Update

   2) Online Update

Update DOs and DON'Ts：

   1) Do not disconnect the power during the update;

   2) If you cannot enter the system due to errors during the update, reset the system through the Recovery Mode;

   3) If you still cannot enter the system through Recovery Mode, please contact us.
